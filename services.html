Services

Sterile Power operates across four strategic service areas — each forming a cornerstone of our sustainable energy platform.

Renewable Energy Brokerage

Sterile Power brokers reliable, cost-effective renewable energy solutions for organizations seeking to decarbonize operations while controlling energy costs. We provide independent, data-driven guidance through every stage — from strategy to signed contract.

Our Services Include:

• Corporate & Virtual Power Purchase Agreements (PPAs):
Structuring and negotiation of long-term renewable energy contracts tailored to your price, volume, and risk appetite.

• Renewable Energy Certificates (RECs):
Sourcing and managing certified RECs for both compliance and voluntary sustainability programs.

• Distributed Generation & On-Site Solar:
Advisory and implementation support for rooftop, carport, or ground-mounted solar systems.

• Battery Energy Storage Advisory:
Sizing, optimization, and integration of energy storage to maximize savings and resilience.

• Energy Risk Management:
Quantitative analysis and hedging strategies to manage price, basis, and imbalance risks.

Why It Matters

We simplify renewable energy procurement — ensuring every transaction is efficient, transparent, and strategically aligned with your sustainability objectives.

Waste-to-Energy (W2E) Solutions

We transform municipal and industrial waste streams into clean, usable energy — helping cities and companies manage waste sustainably while generating electricity and heat.

Our W2E Services Include:

• Project Feasibility & Technical Assessment:
Evaluation of waste feedstock, energy yield potential, and technology suitability.

• Technology Integration:
Matching optimal conversion systems (anaerobic digestion, gasification, or incineration) to specific waste profiles.

• Project Structuring & Development:
Collaboration with municipalities, developers, and financiers to build bankable W2E projects.

• Operations & Maintenance Support:
Advisory for long-term operational performance and environmental compliance.

• Circular Economy Integration:
Integration with recycling and composting programs for maximum environmental benefit.

Why It Matters

W2E projects close the loop between waste management and energy supply — reducing landfill pressure, improving sanitation, and generating sustainable electricity.

Carbon Credit Trading & Emissions Advisory

Sterile Power empowers clients to participate confidently in the global carbon market — monetizing verified emission reductions and supporting compliance or voluntary offsetting goals.

Our Carbon Services Include:

• Carbon Project Development:
Identification and registration of eligible projects (renewables, W2E, reforestation, cookstove programs).

• MRV (Measurement, Reporting, Verification):
Technical assistance to meet Verra (VCS), Gold Standard, and Article 6 criteria.

• Carbon Credit Sales & Brokerage:
Facilitation of carbon credit trades between project developers and buyers globally.

• Offsetting Solutions for Organizations:
Helping companies offset residual emissions through certified, high-integrity credits.

• Policy & Market Advisory:
Advisory for governments and institutions on carbon pricing, trading frameworks, and climate finance.

Why It Matters

Carbon markets unlock climate finance for sustainable projects — turning environmental responsibility into a measurable business advantage.

Energy Equipment Supply & Technical Services

Sterile Power provides end-to-end procurement, supply, and servicing for critical energy infrastructure and equipment across renewables and downstream applications.

Our Services Include:

• Energy Equipment Procurement:
Sourcing and supply of solar panels, inverters, turbines, transformers, and energy storage systems.

• Installation & Commissioning:
Technical supervision and installation of energy equipment by certified professionals.

• Operations & Maintenance (O&M):
Lifecycle support to ensure uptime, efficiency, and performance.

• Vendor and OEM Partnerships:
Collaboration with global manufacturers for quality assurance and after-sales support.

• Energy Efficiency & Audit Services:
Diagnostics and optimization for industries seeking to reduce operational energy costs.

Why It Matters

Reliable equipment and expert servicing are foundational to any sustainable energy strategy — ensuring every project performs as designed, long after commissioning.
