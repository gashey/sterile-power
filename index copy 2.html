About Sterile Power

Sterile Power is a downstream energy services provider with allied interests in renewable energy, waste-to-energy projects, carbon credit trading, and energy equipment procurement.

We combine market expertise, technical depth, and regulatory insight to deliver projects that accelerate Africa's transition to a low-carbon economy — turning waste into power, emissions into opportunity, and energy systems into sustainable value chains.
